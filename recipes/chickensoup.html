Chicken Soup

Description

This wonderful greek-inspired chicken soup is sure to warm your soul.

Taste the flavors of fresh-cut garden vegetables and a hint of lemon for that refreshing zing that only grandma can replicate!

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken with Giblets
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 cup Orzo
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 8 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup of Salt
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste
  • Chopped Parsley, to taste

Steps

  1. Place cold water, salt, whole chicken, and giblets in a large pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, reduce heat to simmer and cook for four hours.
  3. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside to cool.
  4. Strain the broth into a metal bowl, then transfer strained broth back into the pot.
  5. Bring broth to boil and add orzo
  6. Add chopped vegetables, cooking until soft.
  7. Meanwhile, separate chicken meat from bones and carcass, shred, and put aside.
  8. After vegetables are soft, add chicken, lemon juice, parsley, and salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
